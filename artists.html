2E

2E is the leader and one half of the founders of Labrat Records. With two full length studio albums released under his belt Aaron Toohey has already begun etching his name into the Irish Hiphop scene. His first album 'The Lab',managed to secure him placements for small shows and festival slots around the country, most noticeably 'Tayto Park Fest' and 'Live at the NGO'. And with his second studio album, 'Mad Scientist', 2E has managed to keep that momentum going recieving praise from the likes of District Magazine and Nialler9.

ALI

ALI is the youngest member of the Labrat roster and is currently the only other member to have release a project through the label. Her latest single GoodBye featuring 2E earned her spotify playlist placements and its not hard to see why. Bringing a unique blend of smooth Hiphop & RnB mixed with Pop a aesthetic ALIs sound is sure to bob heads. Sitting on a pile of unreleased material ALI is setting up to have a very strong freshman year releasing solo work throughout 2021.

KIDD

Josh K-I-Double-D is the the newst member of the Labrat collective and has been plotting to explode onto the Irish hiphop landscape with the release of his long awaited mixtape 'KIDDOLOGY'. This project features resident beat maker Cian Arthur on production and a handful of different Labrats lending vocals to version tracks on the mixtape. The release will mark KIDDs first ever release as an up and coming artist and is sure to send tremors through the country. There is rumours of a second mixtape that has yet to be named, but is sure to capture the same energy KIDD emits on Kiddology as he pushes himself to prove his writing, flow and delivery with every track he pens.

Yupgrade

Yupgrade AKA Cian Arthur is the coach, house producer and co founder of Labrat Records. Skilled on both the ones and twos and with a pen and pad, Cian is praised by everyone in the collective as the driving force that makes everyone on the label want to push themselves to be better artists. Releasing under the alias of Yupgrade, Cian is currently working on his own EP that is set to come in early 2021. He has recently released the track 'Phoenix',through the Drogheda collective BIFB and has production credits on both 'The Lab' and 'Mad Scientist'. Cian also lent his vocals to the fan favourite 'Trick'.
