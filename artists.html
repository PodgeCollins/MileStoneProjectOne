2E

2E, one half founders of Labrat Records, already has two full length studio albums released under his belt. His first album 'The Lab', managed to secure him placements for small shows and festival slots such 'Tayto Park Fest' and 'Live at the NGO'. His latest album 'Mad Scientist', featuing label mate Yupgrade, has garned attention from Irelands biggest outlet for music news, Nialler9. Niall said he was 'very impressed' with the project and that 'Mars' was his favourite from the tracklist.

ALI

ALI is the youngest member of the Labrat roster and is currently the only othere member to have a project released through the label. Bringing a smooth mix of Pop and Rnb to the table, and some unreleased projects under the hood ALI is setting up to have a very strong freshman year releasing her solo work throughout 2021.

KIDD

KIDD is the the newst member of the collective and has been slowly plotting plans for the release of two seperate mixtapes for the year 2021. The release of the two will be 'Kiddology', souley produced by label mate 'Yupgrade' and feature guest vocals from nearly every member of the collective and more. The second mixtape has yet to be named but is sure to capture the same energy as KIDD pushes himself to prove his writing, flow and delivery with every track he pens.

Yupgrade

Yupgrade is the coach, resident producer for and co founder of Labrat Records. Skilled on both the ones and twos and with a pen and pad, Yupgrade is praised by everyone in the collective as the driving force that makes his label mates want to work harder and be better artists. Yupgrade recently released a track on a compilation tape through BIFB, a collective from Drogheda, in aid of the Black Lives Matter movement. Currently working on his own EP, Yupgrade also has projects in the works with fellow label mate KIDD, so 2021 is looking to be a huge year for the entire collective.
