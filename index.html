About

Labrat Records is an independent record label based in Ashbourne co. Meath. Founded by childhood friends Aaron Toohey, Cian Arthur and Chris Lacy. With their shared love of all things hiphop the three decided to start making and releasing music out of their make shift studio in the back of Chris' shed. With the release of 2es second studio album 'Mad Scientist' the collective has begun to make waves in the Irish Hiphop scene. This website keeps you, the fans, in the know about where you can find their latest shows and helps you stay up to date on their latest projects and interviews.
